About

Sep 16th, 2011

I’m a software consultant with Thoughtworks Australia. Currently, my focus is on iOS development and testing and has been for the last year or so. Who knows how long that will last. I’m also interested in Ruby, including Rails, ReST and a bunch of other things. I’m interested in functional programming without being particularly capable at it. Before Thoughtworks, my background was mainly in scientific software for Earth Science and Astronomy - using mainly C and Java.

Anything that you read on this site, including links to presentations I have given and code I have written are my own thoughts, and not those of my employer.
